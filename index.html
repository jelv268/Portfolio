About Me

Born and raised in Lima, Peru I moved to the US in 2020 to learn how to build cool stuff. Got my aero engineering degree from Purdue in 2024 so that I could call myself a rocket scientist.

I currently focus on solving renewable hydrocarbons at scale, learning from the best along the way. I can machine, weld, code, cut, solder, inject, print, and assemble mostly anything I put my mind to.

Spending half of my free time breaking hardware appart and building things that like to move (intentionally or not) some call robots, and the other half cooking some fire dinner.

I’ve been a surfer for years — always chasing the next wave. Now, I’m chasing the next hardware revolution. Hardware is hard, and that’s exactly why I love it.

I’m looking to break into world-changing engineering startups, where real innovation happens. If you are building something cool, I would love to learn more about it!
